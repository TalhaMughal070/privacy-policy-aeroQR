AeroQR Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

AeroQR respects your privacy. This application is designed to work offline and does not collect or store any personal data on external servers.

1. Data We Collect

AeroQR does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app only processes the following data locally on your device:

  • QR scan results (stored locally on device only)
  • Generated QR codes (saved locally if user chooses)

2. Camera Permission

Camera permission is used only to scan QR codes. All processing happens locally on your device. No camera data is uploaded or shared.

3. Storage Permission

Storage permission is used only to save QR images to your device gallery when you choose to export them.

4. No Personal Data Collection

AeroQR does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Password or sensitive data

5. Internet Usage

The app works offline. Internet is only used when the user opens a link inside a scanned QR code.

6. Third-Party Services

AeroQR does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or data tracking services.

7. Data Security

All data stays on the user’s device. We do not have any servers or remote storage.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app does not collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Any changes will be updated on this page.
