Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

Welcome to AeroQR. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AeroQR collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

AeroQR is designed to work primarily offline and collects minimal user data.

  • QR scan history stored locally on your device
  • Generated QR codes saved locally
  • Optional permissions for camera and storage access

2. Camera Permission

AeroQR requires camera permission to scan QR codes. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

3. Storage Permission

Storage access is used only for saving generated QR images and exporting files to your gallery.

4. No Personal Data Collection

AeroQR does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Passwords
  • Location data

5. Offline Functionality

Most features of AeroQR work completely offline. Internet access may only be used when opening external links scanned from QR codes.

6. Third-Party Services

AeroQR may use trusted third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, or advertisements in future versions. These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We prioritize user privacy and implement reasonable measures to protect locally stored data. However, no digital platform can guarantee complete security.

8. Children's Privacy

AeroQR does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
